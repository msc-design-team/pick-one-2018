Service & Volunteerism

I want to give back to the community!

Giving back to the community is important and Stout has many ways to help you make a difference. Check out various short or long term volunteer opportunities, go by yourself or start a volunteer team with a few friends, or take part in experiential learning through one of your courses for credit. You can also connect with a group of students who are dedicated to service through a student organization. All of these options provide significant learning experiences for students through meaningful service activities that benefit the community.

LGBTQIA+ Programs

I want to support the LGBTQIA+ Community!

The LGBTQIA+ Program Office offers social and educational programming for LGBTQIA+ students and their allies. Participate in the peer mentor program, Open Door discussion, or safe space training. Join GSA, Stout’s official LGBTQIA+ student org. Or simply connect with other students at the Qube, the headquarters for LGBTQIA+ students and allies.

Performing Arts

I want to take the stage!

Explore the performing arts on our campus through theatre, band, choir and dance. These groups provide a creative outlet to students that have enjoyed and developed skills in performing arts and wish to further refine and explore their artistic talents. The band choir and theatre programs are part of academic offerings at Stout, taught by full-time faculty members. The performing arts draw people together and allow them to relate in meaningful and enduring ways.

  • Dance Ensemble
